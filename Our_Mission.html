What Are Trying To Accomplish?

In this website, we are trying to spread awareness about some animals that are becoming extinct Many times we stand back because we think that other people are already doing something about it. However, if we all think the same way than nothing will get done.

With this website, you can donate money to rehabilitation centers where wild animals live because they are sick. You can help feed them and pay for their medical bills.

It doesn't take a lot to help. You don't even have to donate or give money. There are so things that you can do that will even benefit you. If you would like to learn more, go to "Ways To Support". Thank you for even taking the time to look at what you can do for our wildlife. Remember to live wild!
